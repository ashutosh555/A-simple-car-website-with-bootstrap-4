Lamborghini was officially established in 1963 in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy, and the same year debuted its first car, the Lamborghini 350 GTV, a two-seater coupe with a V12 engine. The company's logo featured a bull, a reference to Ferruccio Lamborghini's zodiac sign, Taurus the bull. Lamborghini an international following among car enthusiasts and a reputation for prestige and cutting-edge design.

The company continued to build high-performance cars, including the Murcielago (capable of going over 200 mph), the Gallardo, the Hurracan & the Aventador. Ferruccio Lamborghini died on February 20, 1993, at the age of 76.
